Connect to Roots (C2R) is a non-profit organization conceptualized, managed, and run by a group of Indian with specialization in areas of social, Information Technology, economics, culture, and polity framework.

Through this platform, any individual can contribute to society right now and here from your office/home and start contributing by donating your free time or through donating funds to upliftment of the society. volunteers/donors can connect to their village and towns, select a project of their interest which they can monitor and guide themselves.


We look forward to connecting with you !!
